Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Overview

At AI Business System, we strive to ensure complete satisfaction with our products and services. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for requesting and receiving refunds for our digital products and services.

2. Digital Products

Our digital products are subject to the following refund terms:

  • 30-day money-back guarantee for all digital products
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

3. Subscription Services

For subscription-based services:

  • Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time
  • Pro-rated refunds are available for unused portions of annual subscriptions
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No refunds for partial months of service

4. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • You must not have downloaded or accessed the majority of the content
  • You must provide a valid reason for the refund request
  • You must not have violated our Terms of Service
  • The request must be made within the specified time period

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Customized or personalized products
  • Products that have been downloaded or accessed
  • Services that have been fully delivered
  • Products purchased during promotional periods

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Contact our support team at support@aibusinesssystem.com
  • Include your order number and reason for refund
  • Allow 2-3 business days for review
  • Follow any additional instructions provided by our team

7. Processing Time

Refund processing times:

  • Review period: 2-3 business days
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Bank processing: 3-5 business days (varies by bank)
