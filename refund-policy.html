Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Email us at support@aibusinesssystem.com
  2. Include your order number and reason for refund
  3. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days
  4. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for:

  • Digital products purchased within 30 days
  • Subscription cancellations within 30 days
  • Technical issues preventing product use
  • Duplicate purchases

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Custom implementation services
  • One-on-one consulting sessions
  • Team training sessions
  • Custom development work

5. Subscription Cancellations

For subscription-based services:

  • You can cancel anytime within 30 days for a full refund
  • After 30 days, you can cancel but won't receive a refund
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the billing period
  • No refunds for partial subscription periods

6. Processing Time

Refund processing times:

  • Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
  • PayPal refunds: 1-3 business days
  • Bank transfers: 7-10 business days

7. Contact Us

For any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@aibusinesssystem.com

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24 hours.
